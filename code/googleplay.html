    DAMAGE iD

    DAMAGEiD A-TeamTools
    Everyone
    13
    You don't have any devices.

    DAMAGE iD is a photo-tracking service that provides web and mobile technology solutions to rental companies, loaner fleets and car sharing. Using smartphones or tablets, rental agents follow simple steps to photograph vehicles and gas levels during checkout. Customers review the photos and are prompted to accept Collision Damage waiver. Upon return agents take new photos and flag for damage.

    Damage iD provides before and after photo comparisons to help increase customer service, revenue and efficiency. Additional prompts turn the walk around into a 2nd chance to sell coverage. Photos of fuel levels take the doubt out of charging for gas. Photographic proof lets agents show, not tell, customers about damage or fuel charges. By signing off on photos, customers know they wont be blamed for pre-existing damage.

    Visit www.damageid.com for more information and to begin your subscription.
