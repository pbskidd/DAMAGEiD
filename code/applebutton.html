Description

DAMAGE iD helps you find and prove damage. For vehicle sharing: cars, trucks, RVs , transportation companies and fleets.

Using smartphones or tablets, follow simple steps to photograph vehicles and gas levels, then review photos upon return.

Damage iD provides before and after photo comparisons to help you prove and collect for minor damage. Photographic proof lets agents show, not tell, customers about damage or fuel charges. By signing off on photos, customers know they won’t be blamed for pre-existing damage. All of this ultimately goes to your bottom line.
